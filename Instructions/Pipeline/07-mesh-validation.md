# Mesh validation

Validate the extracted GLB, not the latent mesh.

Hard reject material through-holes, missing major forms, boundary metrics above the asset limit, implausible connected components, or contract lower-profile failure. Treat projection-hole ratio as triage only; it cannot prove closure.

Inspect lit side, rear, three-quarter, and material-close renders. Do not use caps, slabs, generic shells, or broad voxel rebuilds to pass a failed vehicle. Only repair verified microscopic closed export defects.
